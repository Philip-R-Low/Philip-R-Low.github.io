## Websites should work on all platforms
Have you ever tried looking at a restraunt's website on your phone? Maybe you or someone your with has a poorly understood food alergen, gluten for example, and you need to make sure everybody actually can eat there without getting sick. Maybe you want to find the phone number for your favorite pizza place so you can order directly through them instead of using a delivery app. Maybe they're not *on* any of the delivery apps. If you somehow haven't, try it now; this essay isn't going anywhere. Did it look good? Probably not.
